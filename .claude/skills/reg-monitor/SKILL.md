# Regulatory Change Monitor

Search for and summarize recent privacy and compliance regulatory changes.

## Trigger phrases
- /reg-monitor
- "what changed in privacy law"
- "any new regulations"
- "regulatory update for [jurisdiction]"

## What to do

1. Ask which jurisdiction or framework to monitor (or check all):
   - Canada (PIPEDA, Law 25, AIDA)
   - EU (GDPR, AI Act, ePrivacy)
   - US (state privacy laws, FTC actions)
   - Brazil (LGPD)
   - UK (UK GDPR, ICO enforcement)

2. Search via allowed web domains for:
   - New legislation or amendments (last 90 days)
   - Regulatory guidance or opinions
   - Enforcement actions and fines
   - Proposed bills advancing through legislature

3. For each change found, summarize:

**[Change title]**
- Jurisdiction: [country/state]
- Framework: [GDPR/PIPEDA/etc.]
- Status: Enacted / In force / Proposed / Consultation
- Effective date: [date or TBD]
- What changed: [2-3 sentences, plain language]
- Who is affected: [organizations, sectors]
- Action required: [specific steps]

4. Generate a compliance gap report:
   - Map new requirements against existing NorthClaw controls
   - Identify gaps (requirements not yet addressed)
   - Priority: Critical (immediate action) / High (30 days) / Medium (90 days)
   - Recommended actions for each gap

## Do NOT
- Provide legal advice — flag that this is informational, not legal counsel
- Report on changes older than 90 days unless specifically asked
- Include speculation about proposed legislation outcomes
