# AI Governance and EU AI Act Compliance

Classify AI systems, generate conformity documentation, and track compliance timelines.

## Trigger phrases
- /ai-governance
- "classify this AI system"
- "AI Act compliance check"
- "AI risk assessment"

## What to do

1. Collect AI system details:
   - System name and description
   - Purpose and intended use
   - Data inputs (what data does it process)
   - Output type (decisions, recommendations, content, predictions)
   - Autonomy level (fully automated, human-in-the-loop, human-on-the-loop)
   - Deployment region (EU, Canada, US, global)

2. Classify by EU AI Act risk level:

| Level | Criteria | Examples |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Prohibited | Social scoring, real-time biometric ID in public, manipulation | Art 5 |
| High-risk | Employment decisions, credit scoring, law enforcement, education | Annex III |
| Limited | Chatbots, emotion recognition, deepfakes (transparency only) | Art 50 |
| Minimal | Spam filters, AI-enabled games, inventory management | No obligations |

3. For high-risk systems, generate conformity documentation:
   - Risk management system (Art 9)
   - Data governance measures (Art 10)
   - Technical documentation (Art 11)
   - Record-keeping requirements (Art 12)
   - Transparency and information to deployers (Art 13)
   - Human oversight measures (Art 14)
   - Accuracy, robustness, cybersecurity (Art 15)

4. Track against enforcement timeline:
   - Prohibited practices: Feb 2025 (in force)
   - GPAI obligations: Aug 2025 (in force)
   - High-risk (Annex III): Aug 2026
   - Full enforcement: Aug 2027

5. Map to complementary frameworks:
   - ISO 42001 (AI management system)
   - NIST AI RMF (risk management)
   - Canada AIDA (proposed)
   - Quebec Law 25 (automated decision profiling)

6. Generate AI system inventory entry:
   - System ID, name, risk classification
   - Owner, deployment date, review date
   - Data categories processed
   - Compliance status per requirement

7. Board-level governance report:
   - AI system inventory summary
   - Risk distribution (how many systems per level)
   - Compliance gaps and deadlines
   - Resource requirements for remediation
   - Recommended governance structure

## Do NOT
- Classify a system as minimal risk without checking Annex III categories
- Ignore GPAI obligations for foundation model deployments
- Skip Canadian frameworks when deploying in Canada
